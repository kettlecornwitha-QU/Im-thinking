About my Maze Generator

Not too much to say about this one. First, just like with my tensor calculator, the backend is being hosted on a free server, so after about 15 minutes of inactivity, it spins down. This means that the first time you press the “Generate Maze” button, it might take about a minute for the maze to generate, but after that, it should only take a fraction of a second.

Second, setting the branch weight to 0 does not mean no branching. The logic I used to generate the maze makes it almost certain that there will be some branching; increasing the weights simply increases the likelihood that there will be branching during the generation of the maze.
