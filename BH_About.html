About my Maze Generator

This web app displays a number for each square on the Battleship board which corresponds to the likelihood that it is occupied by a ship. You pick a square to shoot in your actual game, which may be a hit or a miss, then you select the same square in the app's board to shoot. The board then updates the scores in each square accordingly.

One thing to keep in mind is that this app is only meant to help guide you in hunting for ships. Once you land a hit, however, use your common sense to shoot adjacent squares until the ship is sunk.

And once again, just like with my other two web apps, the backend is being hosted on a free server, so after about 15 minutes of inactivity, it spins down, so when you first open the web page, it might take about a minute for the backend logic to wake up. Refreshing the page wipes the board clean.
